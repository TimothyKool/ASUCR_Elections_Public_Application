GENERAL ELECTIONS INFORMATION

Each year the ASUCR holds a regular election to choose its Legislative & Executive Branch. All members of the Association, that is all undergraduate and graduate students at UC Riverside, can vote. Regular elections are held the 4th week of Spring Quarter (April 23rd, 2018). Furthermore, all students can vote on referendum & constitutional propositions. Referendum propositions can be placed on the ballot by the ASUCR Senate, Graduate Assembly, or a petition of the student body. Lastly, special elections can occur to recall officials or in some other limited circumstances.

This website is available year-round as a resources to voters, candidates, and proposition proponents/opponents. Please note that the website is usually updated during the winter break for the following elections cycle. All previous elections information and statistic shall be migrated to this website.

For more info, read through this website, check out the comprehensive Elections Bylaws or contact the Elections Committee (all available below).

Voting Period & Locations

The voting period for the 2018 regular election will be from Monday, April 23rd to Wednesday, April 27th. This year voting shall be physical ballots. All voting must be conducted at an ASUCR polling station.

There shall be several on-campuspolling station in a location that will be announced shortly. You must vote on a paper ballot at the polling station. The station will open no later than at 9:00 AM and will close no earlier than at 4:00 PM each day of the voting period. To vote with a paper ballot, you will need either (a) your R-Card or (b) a photo ID and an official document establishing your student ID number.

The regulations related to voting and your rights can be found in  ASUCR Elections Code.
